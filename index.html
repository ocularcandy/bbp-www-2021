Tattoos start at $75. Custom work averages out around $100/hour.
The price goes up based on the amount of time it takes to complete the tattoo.


If you are looking to get larger tattoos like a backpiece or sleeve, you can book 4 hours for $350. All tattoo appointments require a deposit.

Piercings start at $25. We use high quality 316L Surgical Steel jewelry for all initial piercings. ID required for all piercings.

Minors 16-17 MUST bring their birth certificate and some form of ID. Parent must be present with ID.

A deposit is required to set-up a tattoo appointment - this deposit ensures your spot in the calendar, and will come off the final price of the tattoo.


Walk-ins are welcome for piercings, however, if you would like to set-up an appointment, a small deposit will be required.
